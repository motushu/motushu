常见问题

数据会丢失吗？
所有数据都保存在你的设备本地。建议定期使用 App 内的「导出数据」功能备份，以防设备损坏或更换。
为什么收不到打卡提醒？
请在 iOS 设置中确认已允许通知权限，并在 App 内开启打卡提醒、设置提醒时间。
如何恢复数据？
使用 App 内的「导入数据」功能，选择之前导出的备份文件即可恢复。
App 需要联网吗？
不需要。本应用完全离线运行，无需注册账号，也不会上传任何数据。

Frequently Asked Questions

Will my data be lost?
All data is stored locally on your device. We recommend using the "Export Data" feature in the app regularly to back up your records.
Why am I not receiving reminders?
Please make sure notifications are allowed in iOS Settings, and that you have enabled reminders and set a reminder time in the app.
How do I restore my data?
Use the "Import Data" feature in the app and select a previously exported backup file.
Does the app require internet?
No. This app works completely offline. No account registration is required, and no data is uploaded.
